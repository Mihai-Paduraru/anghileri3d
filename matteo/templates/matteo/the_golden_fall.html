THE GOLDEN FALL

Cinematic Team Project

Cinematic sequence produced with Unreal Engine by a team of four people.

For this project I took care of the modeling and texturing of the interiors of the Mayan temple, using the software: Maya, Substance Painter and ZBrush, thus creating the materials and architectural details to immerse the viewer in the atmosphere of the ancient civilization.

A heartfelt thanks to my colleagues – Michele Brez, Gabriele Dallari and Julia Hoa – for the precious collaboration.

Technologies Used

Unreal Engine Maya Substance Painter ZBrush
